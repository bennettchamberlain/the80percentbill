--- C001127: Sheila Cherfilus-McCormick (Democrat, FL, house) ---
Sheila Cherfilus-McCormick is a Democrat representing Florida's 20th Congressional District, a heavily Democratic seat in the Broward and Palm Beach County area that she won in a January 2022 special election to replace the late Alcee Hastings. On the 80% Bill's twenty planks, Cherfilus-McCormick's stated positions read like a progressive wish list. She voted with Biden 100% of the time in the 117th Congress [4] and served on the Foreign Affairs and Veterans' Affairs Committees, where her legislative focus areas included health, housing, and taxation [5]. Cherfilus-McCormick may have campaigned on the kind of populist economic agenda that the 80% Bill embodies, but her tenure in Congress has become a cautionary tale about the gap between progressive rhetoric and personal conduct. Her story is less about where she stands on the issues and more about whether anyone should trust the ground she is standing on.
